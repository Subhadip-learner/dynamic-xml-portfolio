Frequently Asked Questions

Creating your portfolio is easy! Start by signing up for a free account, then choose from our collection of professional templates. Simply fill in your personal information, upload your work samples, and customize the design to match your style. Our intuitive editor makes it simple to add sections for your experience, skills, projects, and contact information. Once you're satisfied with your portfolio, you can publish it with just one click.

Absolutely! PortfolioForge gives you complete ownership of your portfolio. You can download your portfolio files (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and assets) at any time and host them on your preferred hosting provider such as Netlify, Vercel, GitHub Pages, or your own web server. We also provide direct integration with popular hosting platforms for one-click deployment.

Security is our top priority. We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure data centers, regular security audits, and GDPR compliance. Your personal information is never shared with third parties, and you have complete control over your data. We also provide two-factor authentication and regular automated backups to ensure your portfolio is always safe and accessible.

Yes, full customization is available! Our templates are fully customizable - you can change colors, fonts, layouts, add or remove sections, upload custom images, and even add your own CSS for advanced styling. We provide both a visual editor for easy changes and a code editor for developers who want complete control over their portfolio's appearance and functionality.

All our portfolios are fully responsive! Every template is designed to look great on desktops, tablets, and smartphones. We use modern responsive design techniques to ensure your portfolio automatically adapts to any screen size. You can preview how your portfolio looks on different devices using our built-in device simulator before publishing.

We offer flexible pricing for everyone! Start with our free plan that includes basic templates and PortfolioForge subdomain hosting. Our Pro plan ($9/month) includes premium templates, custom domain support, advanced analytics, and priority support. Enterprise plans are available for teams and organizations with additional collaboration features and bulk licensing options.
